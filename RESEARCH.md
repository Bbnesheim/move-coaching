**Purpose:** Central research file for Warp Agents before generating brand, UX, IA, content, or code.

## 1. Project Background  
MOVE Coaching is a personal-brand–driven coaching business founded by **Aline Skancke Olsen**.  
The new site must be built on **Shopify (Dawn)** and serve as:

- Flagship brand platform  
- Digital coaching shop (treningsprogram + kostholdsplaner)  
- Subscription-ready  
- CRM-friendly  
- High-conversion landing environment  

This requirement is explicitly defined in the signed agreement between MOVE Coaching and Nesheim & Vatten (2025).

---

## 2. Goals From Contract  
From the signed agreement and Vedlegg A:  

### Primary Platform Goals  
- Implement full MOVE identity: **logo, colors, fonts, icons, media**  
- Build a **holistic platform**: landing page + shop  
- Strong focus on **conversion** and **scalability**  
- Social proof + storytelling as core brand elements  
- Sell digital products via:  
  - Engangskjøp  
  - Abonnement (4–12 mnd)  
  - Bundles + kampanjepakker  
- Integrate payment providers: **Shopify Payments/Stripe, Klarna, Vipps**  
- SEO-ready + integrated GA4  
- 3D animation for MOVE app  
- Monthly reporting (traffic, salg, anbefalinger)  

### Deliverables Structure (from phases)  
- Research  
- IA + UX  
- Visual design system  
- Shopify theme build  
- Checkout + subscription  
- QA + test launch  
- Growth setup

## 3. Core Brand Insights From Live Site  

### Brand Personality  
- Warm, empathetic, relatable  
- Realistic, no “quick fixes”  
- Expertise grounded in Aline’s personal history  
- Feminine, calm, supportive  
- Strong personal storytelling identity  

### Tone of Voice  
- Direct, motivational, supportive  
- Uses rhetorical questions  
- Conversational and personal (“jeg”, “du”, “vi”)  

### Visual Identity (derived from UI)  
**Color Palette:**

| Usage | Hex |
|-------|------|
| Primary Brown | `#5C4842` |
| Secondary Pink | `#F5B5C4` |
| Background Cream | `#F9F5F0` |
| Text Dark Grey | `#2F2E2B` |

**Typography**  
- Headings: Playfair Display / DM Serif Display  
- Body: Inter / Lato / Open Sans  

**Imagery**  
- Real people, real transformations  
- Family-friendly food images  
- App screenshots  
- Warm beige backgrounds
