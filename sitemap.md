## Home (`/`)

Sections (top to bottom):
- Hero – primary value proposition, portrait of Aline, primary CTA (e.g., “Start nå”) and secondary CTA (“Ta quizen”).
- USP grid – 3–4 key benefits of MOVE Coaching.
- App teaser – short overview of MOVE-appen with 2–3 key features and CTA to App page.
- Founder story teaser – short intro to Aline with link to Aline page.
- Transformations & testimonials – before/after stories, quotes, and proof.
- Programme highlight / pricing teaser – short summary of 6/9/12-mnd pakker with link to Priser/Programmer.
- Quiz entry – prominent CTA and explanation of quiz benefits.
- FAQ teaser – 3–4 common questions with link to full FAQ.
- Email signup / lead magnet (optional) – newsletter or lead capture.
- Footer – navigation, contact, social links, legal links.

## Coaches (`/coaches`)

- Intro section – explanation of coaching philosophy.
- Aline profile – photo, credentials, story summary, link to Aline page.
- Additional coaches (future) – cards with photo, role, short bio.
- CTA section – invite to explore programmes or take quiz.

## App (`/app`)

- Hero – MOVE app value prop and CTA (e.g., “Få tilgang via coachingprogrammet”).
- App features overview – menu planning, family meals, quick recipes, budget, trening med video, wearables, ukentlige check-ins, kursbibliotek.
- Screenshots / gallery – carousel of app UI.
- How app works with coaching – explanation of connection between app and coaching.
- Testimonials focused on app experience.
- CTA – link to recommended programme or prices.

## Priser (`/priser`)

- Hero – clear statement about investment and value.
- Pricing table – 6/9/12-month coaching packages, price per month, total duration, bonuses.
- Comparison section – who each package is best for.
- Included in all packages – bullet list of common benefits.
- Payment info – Klarna, Vipps, Stripe, bindingsperiode.
- FAQ snippet specific to pricing and payment.
- CTA strip – direct links to “Start nå” for each package (to checkout or programme detail).

## Programmer (`/programmer`)

### Programmer overview (`/programmer`)

- Intro – short explanation of MOVE’s approach to programmes.
- Programme cards – one card per 6/9/12-mnd programme with high-level details.
- App access highlight – confirmation that app is included.
- FAQ teaser – link to FAQ.
- CTA – encourage selection of programme or taking the quiz.

### Programme detail (`/programmer/{slug}`)

- Hero – programme name, key promise, primary CTA (“Start nå”), secondary CTA (“Book en prat” or “Ta quizen”).
- Who this is for – persona-aligned description.
- What you get – detailed inclusions (coaching, app, check-ins, support).
- Structure & timeline – 6/9/12-month overview, phases, expectations.
- Before/after & testimonials – specific to this programme where possible.
- Pricing summary – price, billing cadence, any oppstartsgebyr/bonuses.
- FAQ block – programme-specific questions.
- Final CTA – “Start nå” and alternative lead capture.
