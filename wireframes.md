# MOVE Coaching – Text-Based Wireframes

These wireframes describe structure and hierarchy for key pages. They are text-only and theme-agnostic but assume implementation via Shopify Dawn sections.

---

## 1. Home (`/`)

**Section 1 – Hero**
- [Background] Cream or light surface.
- [Left column]
  - H1: Clear promise (e.g., “Langsiktig livsstilsendring med støtte hele veien”).
  - Paragraph: Short explanation referencing tidsklemma, familie, og hverdagen.
  - Primary CTA button: “Start nå” → leads to Priser or recommended programme.
  - Secondary CTA button: “Ta quizen først” → opens/scrolls to quiz flow.
- [Right column]
  - Portrait of Aline or lifestyle image.
  - Optional badge: “6, 9 eller 12 måneder – du velger tempoet.”

**Section 2 – USP Grid**
- H2: “Derfor fungerer MOVE Coaching”.
- 3–4 columns, each with:
  - Icon/illustration.
  - Short heading.
  - 1–2 line description.

**Section 3 – App Teaser**
- Split layout: text + app screenshots.
- H2: “Alt samlet i MOVE-appen”.
- Bulleted list of 4–6 key features.
- CTA: “Se mer om appen” → App page.

**Section 4 – Founder Story Teaser**
- H2: “Fra egen reise til MOVE Coaching”.
- Short paragraph summarising Aline’s story.
- Link/CTA: “Les hele historien til Aline” → Aline page.

**Section 5 – Transformations & Testimonials**
- H2: “Ekte historier. Ekte endring.”
- Slider or grid with 3–6 testimonials:
  - Quote, name/initial, optional before/after thumbnail.

**Section 6 – Programme Highlight / Pricing Teaser**
- H2: “Finn programmet som passer din hverdag”.
- 3 cards for 6/9/12-mnd pakker:
  - Name, short description, price from, key benefits.
  - Button on each: “Se detaljer” → Programme detail or Priser.

**Section 7 – Quiz Entry**
- H2: “Usikker på hvor du skal starte?”
- Paragraph: Explains what quiz helps with.
- Primary button: “Ta quizen” (starts in-page flow or navigates to quiz entry section).

**Section 8 – FAQ Teaser**
- H2: “Ofte stilte spørsmål”.
- 3–4 top questions with collapsible answers.
- Link: “Se alle spørsmål” → FAQ.

**Section 9 – Email Signup / Lead Capture (optional)**
- H3: “Få inspirasjon rett i innboksen”.
- Short copy and email input + submit button.

**Section 10 – Footer**
- Logo, short description.
- Navigation links.
- Social icons.
- Legal links.

## 3. App (`/app`)

**Section 1 – Hero**
- H1: “MOVE-appen – verktøyet som følger deg i hverdagen”.
- Paragraph: Emphasise convenience, family-friendly, realistic.
- CTA: “Få tilgang via coachingprogrammet” → Programmer/Priser.

**Section 2 – Feature Grid**
- H2: “Dette får du i appen”.
- Grid of features:
  - Build your own menu.
  - Family-friendly and quick recipes.
  - Budget-friendly alternativer.
  - Trening med videoøvelser.
  - Wearables integrasjon.
  - Ukentlige check-ins.
  - Kursbibliotek.

**Section 3 – Screenshots / Gallery**
- Carousel with 3–6 app screens (menu, recipes, workouts, check-ins).

**Section 4 – How App + Coaching Work Together**
- 3-step visual:
  1. Velg program.
  2. Få tilgang og personlig oppfølging.
  3. Bruk appen i hverdagen.

**Section 5 – Testimonials (App-Focused)**
- Quotes specifically about app convenience.

**Section 6 – CTA Strip**
- Single row with copy: “Klar for å bruke appen i din hverdag?”
- Button: “Se priser og programmer”.

## 4. Priser (`/priser`)

**Section 1 – Hero**
- H1: “Velg et løp som passer din hverdag”.
- Paragraph: Explains 6/9/12-mnd as different nivåer av forpliktelse.

**Section 2 – Pricing Table**
- 3 columns: 6 mnd, 9 mnd, 12 mnd.
- Each column:
  - Package name.
  - Price per month.
  - Total length.
  - Highlights/bonuses (e.g., gratis oppstart + ekstra måned for 12 mnd).
  - Primary CTA: “Start nå”.
- Mark recommended plan visually.

**Section 3 – What’s Included in All Pakker**
- H2 + bullet list (coaching, app access, check-ins, støtte, etc.).

**Section 4 – Which Package is Right for You?**
- Short guidance with 3 columns mapping personas to packages.

**Section 5 – Payment & Practical Info**
- Text + icons for Stripe/Klarna/Vipps.
- Short FAQs about betaling, bindingstid, kansellering.

**Section 6 – FAQ Snippet**
- 3–5 pricing-related FAQs.

**Section 7 – Final CTA**
- Banner with single strong CTA to recommended programme.

## 10. Shared Components

- **Header**: Logo, nav, mobile menu, primary CTA (e.g., “Se programmer”).
- **Footer**: Logo, nav links, contact, social, legal links.
- **Quiz Entry Pattern**: Reusable block with headline, short text, and CTA button.
- **CTA Strips**: Full-width sections with short copy and 1–2 buttons used across pages.
